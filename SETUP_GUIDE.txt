Gmail App Password Setup Guide — Msoco Rockers Contact Form
============================================================

The contact form at /weddings/contact sends emails via Gmail SMTP.
Two env vars are needed: GMAIL_USER and GMAIL_APP_PASSWORD.

STEP 1: Enable 2-Step Verification (required for App Passwords)
----------------------------------------------------------------
1. Go to https://myaccount.google.com/security
2. Under "How you sign in to Google", click "2-Step Verification"
3. Follow the prompts to enable it (you'll need your phone)
4. Once enabled, you'll see a green checkmark next to "2-Step Verification"

STEP 2: Generate an App Password
---------------------------------
1. Go to https://myaccount.google.com/apppasswords
   (If this link doesn't work, search "App Passwords" in your Google Account settings)
2. Under "Select app", type a name like "Msoco Website"
3. Click "Create"
4. Google will show a 16-character password (e.g., "abcd efgh ijkl mnop")
5. Copy this password — you won't be able to see it again

STEP 3: Add to .env
--------------------
Open /Users/morena/Software/msocorockers/web/.env and fill in:

  GMAIL_USER=your-actual-gmail@gmail.com
  GMAIL_APP_PASSWORD=abcdefghijklmnop

Notes:
- Remove spaces from the app password (Google shows it with spaces)
- Do NOT use your regular Gmail password — only the App Password works
- The .env file is gitignored and will not be committed

STEP 4: Test
------------
1. Run the dev server: npm run dev
2. Go to http://localhost:3000/weddings/contact
3. Fill in the form and submit
4. Check your Gmail inbox — you should receive the inquiry email
5. The person who submitted should also receive a confirmation email

TROUBLESHOOTING
---------------
- "Invalid login" error: Make sure 2-Step Verification is ON and you're
  using the App Password, not your regular password.
- "Less secure app access" is NOT needed — App Passwords bypass that.
- If App Passwords option doesn't appear: 2-Step Verification must be
  enabled first. It can take a few minutes to appear after enabling.
